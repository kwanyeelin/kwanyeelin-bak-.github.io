About Me

I will be joining CUHK / Multimedia Lab as a Post-Doctoral Researcher in Spring 2020, advised by Prof. Xiaogang Wang and Prof. Hongsheng Li. I am now a Senior Researcher at SenseTime Research. Before join SenseTime, I received my PhD degree in the Department of Information Science, School of Mathematical Sciences, Peking University in 2019. My research interests include computer vision, image processing, with focus on 3d scene understanding, human pose estimation, and deep generative models.
