About Me

I am currently a Ph.D candidate in the Department of Information Science, School of Mathematical Sciences, Peking University. I am also a research intern at SenseTime Research. My research interests include computer vision, image processing, with focus on perceptual metrics, human pose estimation, and deep generative models.
